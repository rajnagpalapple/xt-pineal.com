Here is a list of major Pineal XT Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits:

Iodine

Iodine is a vital mineral essential for thyroid function and metabolic regulation. It plays a crucial role in brain development and is necessary for the production of thyroid hormones, which are important for maintaining energy levels and supporting overall metabolic activity.

Alma Extract

Alma Extract, derived from the Indian gooseberry, is renowned for its high vitamin C content and potent antioxidant properties. It is traditionally used to enhance immune function, improve skin health, and promote longevity due to its rejuvenating effects.
3

Chaga Mushroom

Chaga Mushroom is a type of fungus that grows on birch trees and is rich in a complex array of bioactive compounds. It has been used in folk medicine for centuries, primarily for boosting immunity and overall vitality, and is believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits.
4

Schisandra Powder

Schisandra Powder comes from a berry that is used in traditional Chinese medicine, known for its adaptogenic properties that help the body resist stressors of all kinds, whether physical, chemical, or biological.
5

Turmeric

Turmeric is a widely used spice, celebrated for its curcumin content, which has powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. It has been linked to a variety of health benefits, including the potential to improve heart health and prevent against certain neurological conditions.
6

Chlorella Powder

Chlorella Powder is a type of green algae that is a rich source of proteins, vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber. It's often used as a detox agent to bind with heavy metals and other toxins to facilitate their removal from the body.
7

Burdock Powder

Burdock Powder is made from the root of the burdock plant and is traditionally used for blood purification. It contains powerful antioxidants such as phenolic acids, quercetin, and luteolin, which are known to support skin health and increase blood circulation.
